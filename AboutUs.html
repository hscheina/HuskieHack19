Fox Valley Girls Coding CLub presents you with a website made for girls and women looking into or need help in a STEM field. Being women, we face discrimination for our love for technology. With this, it motivated us to create a place where another women can go to get help and be encouraged about STEM careers.

Meghan Lu is a junior at St. Charles North. She plans to major in computer science yet can barely turn on a computer. Her AP Computer Science experience has taught her that you really can cram for an AP test in one day-an important life lesson. Her hopes and dreams include becoming more active in social activism, sleeping ASAP, and that no one ever reads this about me section. She hopes that you enjoyed this website and that it made you think about gender disparity in CS, which is unfortunately still a very real and upsetting issue in 2019.
