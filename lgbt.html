LGBTQ+ in STEM

LGBTQ+ members have a harder time with bullying and harassment. Unfortunetly this is no different for STEM majors. But, over the years, the numbers have been climbing.

These resources include why is is hard to be LGBT in STEM, personal accounts and community support, and how being queer can help you in school.
