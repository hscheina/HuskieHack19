Stereotyping and Myths

According to Rasmussen College, the top 5 myths/sterotypes that women in computer science are:

  1. Computer Science doesn't help people
  2. Men are better quantitative thinkers than women
  3. Computer Science means working in isolation
  4. Women's ideas are unwelcome
  5. You must know how to code to work in technology

But it is important to note that teach simply are not true. Women and men use computers, therefore womens input is just as important as mens. These resources are here to explain and break the myths and sterotypes that women believe to be ture if they are going to join the field of STEM
